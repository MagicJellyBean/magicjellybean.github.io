My projects

Autonomous ball collecting robot

As part of my diploma thesis to complete my engineering studies in the field of Automation and Robotics, I made a project and a prototype of an autonomous robot for collecting balls. The project turned out to be a big challenge and allowed me to develope many skills. The design of individual elements and the assembly were made in Fusion 360, and then all elements were printed using 3D printing technology. The central unit of the robot is the ESP32 module, and the component responsible for image detection is ESP32-CAM. In order for the robot to operate efficiently, the computing power of the computer and the program written in the PYTHON language were also used.

3D printer

In 2018 I purchased the Anet A8 printer. I liked 3D printing so much that currently there is not much left of the original printer. Thanks to continuous improvement, I have learned a lot. Currently i am designing my own components to improve my printer or for my own use.

Mini weather station

A device that collects data from temperature and humidity sensors, both inside and outside. It has been equipped with a time module and an LCD display which displays the current time, temperature and air humidity. It is also possible to turn off the display backlight with a switch.

I found a LED cube project on the internet once and told myself that I want to do it and find out how it works. That's what happened, I did the necessary purchases, submitted the entire project and started writing the program. The program consisted of displaying several different sequences in succession, creating a certain animation.
